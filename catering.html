Catering

More About Catering

At the Mint we cherish a passion for creative combinations of healthy foods with delightfully rich flavours. We create delicious balanced meals incorporating local produce and ancient grains connecting our patrons to health, freshness, and peace. The Mint crafts all the vibrant, flavourful marination and dressings inhouse and sources produce ethically. Quality ingredients picked and prepared by our chefs are carefully packed for your convenience to shape mindful eating experiences in your office or at the comfort of your home with friends and families. Our aim is to create a transcendent meal experience.




Catering policy

COVID19
Due to COVID19 individual packed food with cutlery and water will be avilable for corporate caterings.
Dietary
when a meal package is ordered for a group and a guest has a food allergy and/or is gluten free/vegetarian/vegan, this guest will be accommodated at no additional charge as long as the alternate menu item(s) is/are chef’s choice. Each menu item in the package will be accommodated for this guest.
Custom requests and menus .
Custom menus based on client needs are available upon request and pricing will be according to requests. Custom items may be subject to additional lead time and requests should be made well ahead of event. Our chef will be pleased to provide custom menus based on budget and themes as required.
Ordering lead time
Orders must be placed and confirmed at least 5 working days in advance of the event. However, we will try our best to accommodate last minute requests. Please speak with our manager to determine what can be offered for last minute requests.
Payment
To confirm the catering request, 50% of total amount needs to be paid in advance. We do accept debit card, credit card, cash & cheque payment options.
Minimums
Minimum catering order is $750 and does not include taxes and service. We are aware this new minimum order may be difficult to reach for smaller events, and as such our team has established partner vendors who are able to provide high quality, reliable service at lower minimums. Our team can do the food delivery onsite with a delivery fee.
Cancellations
We realize that emergency cancellations do occur and will do our best to avoid any cancellation charges. If an order is cancelled with at least 5 working days’ notice, no cancellation charges will be applied except for specialty ordered food products or prepared foods that cannot otherwise be utilized. Less than 3 working days notice will be charged 100%.

Don't see what you are looking for? Contact our Catering Manager and we will develop a customized menu that fits your needs.
