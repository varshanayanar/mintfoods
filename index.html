Who are we ?

At the Mint we cherish a passion for creative combinations of healthy foods with delightfully rich flavours. We create delicious balanced meals incorporating local produce and ancient grains connecting our patrons to health, freshness, and peace. The Mint crafts all the vibrant, flavourful marination and dressings inhouse and sources produce ethically. Quality ingredients picked and prepared by our chefs are carefully packed for your convenience to shape mindful eating experiences in your office or at the comfort of your home with friends and families. Our aim is to create a transcendent meal experience.
